A design pattern is a general, reusable solution to a commonly occurring problem in
software design. The Gang of Four catalog groups patterns into three categories:
Creational (e.g. Singleton, Factory Method, Builder), Structural (e.g. Adapter,
Decorator, Composite), and Behavioral (e.g. Observer, Strategy, Command).
